# LAW THAT MODIFIES LAW 31557, LAW THAT REGULATES THE EXPLOITATION OF REMOTE GAMES AND REMOTE SPORTS BETTING, TO SPECIFY CONCEPTS AND ITS SCOPE

### Article 1. Modification of articles 3, 7, 8, eleven, 12, 13, fifteen, 22, 24, 26, 29, 31, 38 and 40, and of the first and seventh final complementary provisions of the Law 31557, Law that regulates the exploitation of remote gaming and remote sports betting.

## AMENDING SUPPLEMENTARY PROVISION

### ONLY. Modification of article 243-C of the Penal Code

Article 243-C of the Penal Code, approved by Legislative Decree 635, is modified in the following terms:

### Article 243-C.- Illegal operation of casino games and slot machines or games or remote sports betting

He who organizes, conducts or operates casino games and slot machines, or games or sports bets at a distance without having complied with the requirements demanded by the laws and their regulations for its organization, management or exploitation, will be repressed with a custodial sentence of no less than one nor more than four years, with a three hundred and sixty-five day fine and disqualification from carrying out said activity, in accordance with subsection 4) of article 36 of the Code.

Dear Sir or Madam:

I am pleased to greet you and at the same time inform you that a service has been implemented to provide the information of the Registry of Persons Prohibited from Accessing Establishments for the Operation of Casino Games and Slot Machines, for the holders of authorizations to operate technological platforms for remote gaming and remote sports betting.

In order to access the information of the Registry of Persons Prohibited from Accessing Establishments for the Exploitation of Casino Games and Slot Machines, the Holder must enter through the Prior Communications module of the MINCETUR Extranet:

- Description of the fixed pubic IP(s) of the Registrant.

Due to security policies, only up to three (03) fixed public IPs per Company will be accepted. In case of updating or modification of IPs, a new list of IPs, maximum three (03), must be entered through the Prior Communications module of the Extranet. The previously registered list will be left without effect.

MINCETUR will provide a service, through which you can access the information of the list of persons prohibited from accessing gaming rooms and/or platform, in json format and will be available to those registered IPs, according to the following structure:

The URL to access the service is as follows:

https://wsel.mincetur.gob.pe/wsludopatia/api/RegistroLudopatia/credencial/consultarRegistroLudopatia

Where the word credential found in the URL of the service, must be replaced by the credentials of the Holder.

To obtain the credential, the Holder must enter the MINCETUR extranet (https://extranet.mincetur.gob.pe/) and go to the option "Manage data", at the end of this form you will see the button "View Credential", click on it and you will be able to view your credentials.

Finally, the information of the Registry of Persons Prohibited from Accessing Establishments Intended for the Operation of Casino Games and Slot Machines (Remote Gaming and Sports Betting) will be updated every day at 11:00 pm. It should be noted that only the Registers that are in active status at the time of the update will be shown.
